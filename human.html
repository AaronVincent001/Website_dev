The Human Alliance

The Heroes Archmage: A wizard riding a horse. He is usually picked first because he has an aura that constantly refills everyone's mana (magic power). He can also summon water elementals and rain down ice blizzards. Mountain King: A tough dwarf warrior. He is great at fighting up close and knocking out single targets by throwing a storm hammer at them. Paladin: A holy knight who focuses on defense. He can heal your units, make himself completely invincible for a short time, and bring dead troops back to life. Blood Mage: A fire wizard who can summon a phoenix, burn areas of the map, and steal magic from enemies to give it to your teammates. The Buildings Main Base & Economy Town Hall (Keep / Castle): Your main base where you train workers (Peasants). If you are attacked, you can ring a bell here to temporarily turn your workers into a militia army to defend the base. Farm: Gives you food so you can build a bigger army. Altar of Kings: The place where you hire and revive your heroes. Troop Production & Upgrades Barracks: Trains your basic foot soldiers, riflemen, and knights. Blacksmith: Where you upgrade the weapons and armor of your army. Lumber Mill: Where workers drop off wood and where you upgrade building strength. Arcane Sanctum: Trains your magic units, like Priests (who heal) and Sorceresses (who slow down enemies). Workshop: Builds mechanical units like flying machines, mortar teams, and tanks. Gryphon Aviary: Trains your powerful flying units, like Gryphon Riders. Arcane Vault: A shop where your heroes can buy healing potions and items. Defensive Towers You start by building a basic Scout Tower, which you can upgrade into three different types: Guard Tower: Shoots fast arrows at ground and air targets. Cannon Tower: Shoots heavy cannonballs that blast whole groups of ground enemies. Arcane Tower: Burns the magic (mana) out of attacking enemies and reveals invisible units.
